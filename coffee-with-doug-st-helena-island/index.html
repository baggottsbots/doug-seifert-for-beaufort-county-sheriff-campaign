About the Event

Coffee, Conversation, and a Real Plan for North of the Broad

Join Doug Seifert for coffee and conversation at Lowcountry Cider Co. & Superior Coffee Sea Island on Wednesday, May 20, from 8:00 to 9:00 AM. This is an informal, open-to-the-public gathering on St. Helena Island where residents can hear Doug’s plans for law enforcement north of the Broad, ask questions, and meet with neighbors and community members.

Doug Seifert has spent 20 years inside the Beaufort County Sheriff’s Office, working the road, leading investigations, and serving as Lieutenant of the Southern Enforcement Branch. Now he’s running for Beaufort County Sheriff in the Republican Primary on June 9, 2026.

Come early, grab a cup of coffee, and let’s talk about the future of Beaufort County.

What you’ll hear about

  • Doug’s plan for law enforcement north of the Broad
  • Stronger community engagement and trust
  • Deputy recruitment, retention, and competitive pay
  • Targeted enforcement against violent crime
  • Real-time help for residents facing fraud and scams
  • Smarter traffic enforcement in a growing county
