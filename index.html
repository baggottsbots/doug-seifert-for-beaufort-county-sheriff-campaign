About Doug

A Career Built on Service

Doug Seifert isn’t a newcomer to public safety — he’s spent 20 years inside the Beaufort County Sheriff’s Office, rising through the ranks from frontline enforcement to Lieutenant of the Southern Enforcement Branch.

When longtime Sheriff P.J. Tanner announced he would not seek reelection after nearly three decades of service, Doug was ready. He filed his candidacy just two minutes after the filing period opened on March 16, 2026 — because this has always been his goal.

Before Beaufort, Doug served as a Town Councilman in North Bend, Ohio, where he developed his foundation in civic leadership and public accountability. That experience, combined with two decades of boots-on-the-ground law enforcement, makes him uniquely prepared for the role.

A proud Hilton Head Republican and Lowcountry community member, Doug is committed to building on Sheriff Tanner’s legacy while bringing the fresh perspective this department deserves.

Campaign Priorities

Doug’s Plan for Beaufort County

Competitive Deputy Pay

Beaufort County deserves the best deputies available. Doug will push for higher salaries to attract and retain qualified professionals — filling vacancies with talent, not compromise.

Youth Violence Prevention

Reactive policing isn’t enough. Doug will implement proactive community-based programs to address the root causes of youth violence before they escalate into crises.

Traffic & Drug Enforcement

Our roads and communities deserve focused attention. Doug will strengthen both traffic enforcement and drug interdiction to make Beaufort County safer for every resident and visitor.

Fresh Leadership

Sheriff Tanner built a well-oiled machine. Doug’s goal is to honor that legacy while bringing new energy and ideas — because standing still is never an option in public safety.
