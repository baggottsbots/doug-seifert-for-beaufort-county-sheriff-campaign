About the Event

Lunch, Conversation, and a Real Plan for Beaufort County

Come have lunch with Doug Seifert and meet the candidate in a relaxed community setting at Lowcountry Produce in Seabrook on Friday, May 22, from 11:30 AM to 2:00 PM. This is an opportunity to ask questions, share concerns, and hear Doug’s vision for public safety and law enforcement in Beaufort County.

Doug Seifert has spent 20 years inside the Beaufort County Sheriff’s Office, working the road, leading investigations, and serving as Lieutenant of the Southern Enforcement Branch. Now he’s running for Beaufort County Sheriff in the Republican Primary on June 9, 2026.

Sandwiches and lunch items will be available for purchase. We look forward to seeing you there.

What you’ll hear about

  • Stronger community engagement and trust across Beaufort County
  • Deputy recruitment, retention, and competitive pay
  • Targeted enforcement against violent crime
  • Real-time help for residents facing fraud and scams
  • Smarter traffic enforcement in a growing county
  • Focused, accountable drug enforcement strategy
