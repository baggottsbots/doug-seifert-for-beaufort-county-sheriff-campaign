A Legacy of Leadership

Sheriff P.J. Tanner has served the Beaufort County community with distinction for nearly three decades. His commitment to public safety, integrity, and community-oriented policing has set the standard for law enforcement in our region.

After nearly 30 years of dedicated service, Sheriff Tanner announced he would not seek reelection, opening the door for the next generation of leadership. His legacy is one of professionalism, accountability, and unwavering dedication to the citizens of Beaufort County.

Throughout his tenure, Sheriff Tanner built a department rooted in trust, transparency, and community partnership. He has mentored countless officers, including Lieutenant Doug Seifert, who rose through the ranks under his leadership.

Sheriff Tanner’s endorsement of Doug Seifert reflects his confidence in Doug’s ability to continue the tradition of excellence and service that has defined his administration.
